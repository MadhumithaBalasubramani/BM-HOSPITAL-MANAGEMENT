OPD MANAGEMENT

OPD (Out-Patient Department) is responsible for managing out-patients who just come for a doctor for their issue/ disease checkup and managing front desk operations.

OPD is the first intermediary between a patient and the hospital staff.

A patient if first taken to the OPD for inspection, then the doctors from the OPD refers them to the respective departments of treatment the patient needs.
